One Small Step

(Hourse Frontier)

A small landing page to better show off the project.

One Small Step came from the quote from Armstrong during his moon landing, as I felt the nature of the game was much as he had intended his quote to be. Initially, I had been thinking of something involving "leap", but then I thought of One Small Step.

Download the latest build here , no guarantees that it's stable. (Look for the ship.zip file)
